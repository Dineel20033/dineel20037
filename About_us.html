About Us

"Metro Care Hospital is dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services with a focus on patient-centered care and advanced medical technologies. Since our inception, we have been committed to delivering the highest standards of medical excellence, compassionate care, and innovative treatments. Our team of experienced healthcare professionals works tirelessly to create a safe, supportive, and healing environment for all our patients. At Metro Care Hospital, your health and well-being are our top priorities".

Our Vision

"Our vision is to be the leading healthcare provider known for our unwavering commitment to quality care, innovation, and community wellness. We aspire to transform healthcare by making advanced medical treatments accessible and ensuring that every patient receives the best possible care. At Metro Care Hospital, we envision a healthier world where healthcare is not just a service, but a trusted partnership".

Our Mission

"Our mission at Metro Care Hospital is to enhance the lives of our patients by providing comprehensive, patient-focused care with integrity, respect, and compassion. Our Locations

Metro Care Hospital is dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare across our three locations. Our Colombo branch offers central access to advanced medical services, while our Kandy branch is known for its comprehensive care and modern facilities. The Galle branch serves the community with high-quality care and a focus on overall well-being. Each location is staffed with experienced professionals committed to delivering personalized and compassionate care.

Colombo Branch

Our Colombo branch is centrally located, offering convenient access to top-notch medical services. With a team of highly skilled professionals and state-of-the-art facilities, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive care for all your health needs. Visit us for expert consultations, diagnostic services, and personalized treatment plans.

Kandy Branch

Nestled in the heart of Kandy, our Kandy branch is renowned for its commitment to exceptional patient care. We offer a wide range of medical services, from routine check-ups to specialized treatments. Our caring staff and modern facilities ensure a comfortable and efficient healthcare experience for every patient.

Galle Branch

Our Galle branch provides high-quality medical care with a focus on community health and well-being. Located conveniently for residents and visitors alike, we offer a range of services including preventive care, diagnostic services, and expert medical consultations. Our dedicated team is here to support you and your family's health needs.
