Doctors page

Dr.Samantha Green

Dr. Samantha Green is a seasoned cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in treating complex heart conditions. She graduated from Johns Hopkins University and completed her residency at the Cleveland Clinic. Dr. Green is known for her compassionate approach and her commitment to patient education, ensuring her patients are well-informed about their heart health.

Dr.Rohan De Silva

Dr. Rohan De Silva is an accomplished orthopedic surgeon specializing in joint replacement and sports injuries. With a degree from the University of Melbourne and a fellowship in minimally invasive surgery, he brings advanced surgical techniques to Metro Care Hospital. Patients appreciate his meticulous care and his dedication to helping them regain mobility and quality of life.

Dr.Harsha Fernando

Dr. Harsha Fernando is a neurologist known for his expertise in treating neurological disorders such as epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, and Parkinson’s disease. He studied at King’s College London and completed his residency in neurology at the National Hospital for Neurology and Neurosurgery in London. Dr. Fernando is highly regarded for his patient-centric approach and his focus on integrating the latest research into his practice.

Dr.Kavindi Perera

Dr. Kavindi Perera is a dedicated pediatrician with a passion for providing comprehensive care to children from infancy through adolescence. She earned her medical degree from the University of Colombo and completed her residency at Lady Ridgeway Hospital for Children. Known for her gentle manner and thorough approach, Dr. Perera strives to make every visit comfortable and informative for both her young patients and their parents.

Dr.Nuwan Jayasinghe

Dr. Nuwan Jayasinghe is an experienced obstetrician and gynecologist with a focus on maternal-fetal medicine. He graduated from the University of Peradeniya and completed his residency at the National Hospital of Sri Lanka. Dr. Jayasinghe is skilled in managing high-risk pregnancies and minimally invasive gynecological surgeries, and he is deeply committed to improving women’s health outcomes.

Dr.Julian Roberts

Dr. Julian Roberts is an oncologist with a focus on breast and colorectal cancers. He graduated from the University of Toronto and pursued a fellowship in oncology at the MD Anderson Cancer Center. Dr. Roberts is known for his empathetic approach to cancer care, guiding patients and their families through challenging diagnoses with clarity and support. He is actively involved in clinical trials and research, aiming to bring cutting-edge treatments to Metro Care Hospital.
